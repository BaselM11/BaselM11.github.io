2023 24th International Middle East Power System Conference (MEPCON)

Title: "A Novel Short Electric Load Forecasting Approach Using a Multivariate Transformer Neural Network and CEEMDAN"
This study proposes a novel short-term electric load forecasting approach that leverages CEEMDAN decomposition for feature extraction and Transformer Neural Networks (TRNNs) for prediction. The proposed method demonstrates superior performance compared to established techniques like vanilla TRNN, CEEMDAN-LSTM, SARIMA, and SARIMAX, highlighting its effectiveness in handling the complex non-linearities of load data.
DOI: 10.1109/MEPCON58725.2023.10462386.
